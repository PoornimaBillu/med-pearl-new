Contextualized Patient Reports and Alerts

What can healthcare organizations meaningfully do to address the epidemic of deep discouragement and burnout so many of our clinicians are experiencing? Provide a single screen for medical knowledge and patient data. Using MedPearl improves time to determine the best quality management and workup including ordering of first-line labs, imaging, and treatment. As a result, patients with undifferentiated complex problems arrive at the specialist’s exam room having completed the work-up and first-line therapy that would have otherwise occurred at the first specialty visit. Patients ultimately referred, then, are those most likely to need surgery or other interventions performed only by the specialist.

Fully customizable to address your needs

The MedPearl Content Creator platform is a unique no-code environment enabling clinicians to create custom guides and algorithms augmented by the intelligent contextualization of patient data from any EMR. Advanced AI drives continuous improvement of the platform, curated by your clinician content creation and governance team if your healthcare system wishes to create your own library. And being EMR- and content management-agnostic, you can create your own MedPearl. Without programmers or EMR analyst resources.
